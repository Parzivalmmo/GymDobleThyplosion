The Team

General Strategy

When you are in front of the gym leader, pop an Amulet Coin / Riches Charm and initiate interactions with the gym leaders to battle them. Note that they have a cooldown of 18 hours. After battling them, heal afterwards before battling the next gym leader.

You can use the extra slot to level one Pokemon with an Exp. Share / Exp. Amplifier.

If you would like to advance into a better gym run strategy, refer to this document.

FAQ

Do the Typhlosions need to have 31 Special Attack IV and 31 Speed IV?
Just about 21 Speed IVs should suffice, and 31 SpA IVs are very important to do the maximum damage rolls. However, when you don’t want to do Gym Runs anymore and want to sell them, people would prefer looking at 31 IVs on both the important natures. This is for the long term just in case you prefer to not sell.
Do the Typhlosions need to be Modest? Can you use Mild and Rash natures too?
Modest is what the players usually look at the market, but Mild and Rash nature also works the same in your favor. The answer is generally the same.
Do the Typhlosions really need to be Lvl 100s?
Yes, because the gym’s scale at the highest level of your Pokemon, and their payout also scales from there. Generally, sources say Lvl 90+.
Can I level up my Pokemon during my gym run?
Generally, I would advise to only have one Pokemon levelled, as the text boxes will consume time.
